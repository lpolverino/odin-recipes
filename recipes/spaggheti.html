Spaggheti

A simple recipe to cook spagghetti with tomato souce, very easy to cook

It's no requiered any expierece to prepare this comun plate

Ingredients

  • 100gr of your favorite spaggheti
  • 100ml of tomate souce
  • Salt, to like
  • Oregan

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add the spagghetti in the boiling water, and turn the heat to min
  3. Heat the souce
  4. add oregan to the souce once you turn out the stove
  5. merge and enjoy!
