Spaggheti

A very fulled omellete with a simple coction for anybody with a stove and ingredients to prepare

Ingredients

  • 2 o 3 eggs
  • 1 tomato
  • 3 alabaca leaf
  • 50gr of muzarrela
  • Oregan

Steps

  1. blend the eggs
  2. throw the liquid in an frying pan
  3. when the first side is cooked, put the cheese inside and fold the omellete trapping the cheese inside
  4. cook until there is no more liquid egg
  5. cut the tomato in slices and put it over the omellete with the albaca leaf
  6. enjoy
